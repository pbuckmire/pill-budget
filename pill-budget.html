⚡ PILL Principle: Unlike snowball/avalanche, the PILL method focuses on interest cancellation at the amortization level — applying small prepayments directly to principal at the right moment to eliminate disproportionately large future interest charges. Debts are ranked by interest cost per dollar owed (APR × balance), not just highest rate. Your available prepay pool is distributed starting with Priority 1 until its minimum is covered, then cascades. Estimates are illustrative; actual savings depend on your lender's prepayment application policy.
